Cherry Blossom Sakura Season

Welcome to Japan’s pink and modern world of cherry blossoms.

Japan’s cherry blossom trees, or sakura, are top attractions for travellers, but it can be a challenge to perfectly time a trip to see their fleeting beauty.

the Japan Meteorological Corporation (JMC) released a new cherry blossom forecast for the year, helping travellers eager to see the ephemeral blossoms decide when to travel. The JMC regularly updates its estimates of when the blossoms will begin to flower – and reach full bloom – in about 1000 viewing locations through the country, based on cherry tree growth status, temperature averages and past data for each area.
